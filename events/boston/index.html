Directions
First Church is located at 66 Marlborough St., Boston, MA 02116, at the corner of Berkeley and Marlborough Streets, a short walk from the Arlington St. or Copley Sq. T Stops on the Green Line. The building is fully handicap accessible with ramps, an elevator, handicap bathrooms and designated parking spots. Parking can be found at the Boston Common Garage.

By subway (MBTA):
Green Line: Copley
Orange Line: Back Bay Station (also Commuter Rail)
Red Line: Charles/MGH

By car:
Boston Common Garage
Entrance on Charles St.

Back Bay Garage
500 Boylston St.
Entrance on St. James and Clarendon St.

Both parking garages are a 5-10 minute walk from First Church. Limited metered parking on surrounding streets.

Buy Tickets

Tickets will be held in your name at the door. We apologize but we don’t send out physical tickets.
